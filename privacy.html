Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-05-05

English

Gap Pin is designed as a simple Apple Watch game. We aim to keep data collection to a minimum.

  • The app stores gameplay progress such as high score locally on your device.
  • The app does not require an account or sign-in.
  • The app does not intentionally collect personal data.
  • If future features introduce data collection, this policy will be updated accordingly.

简体中文

《见缝插针 for Watch》是一款 Apple Watch 小游戏，我们尽量将数据收集控制在最少。

  • 游戏分数、最高分等进度信息仅保存在你的设备本地。
  • 应用不需要账号登录。
  • 应用不会主动收集个人身份信息。
  • 如果未来新增会涉及数据收集的功能，我们会同步更新本隐私政策。
